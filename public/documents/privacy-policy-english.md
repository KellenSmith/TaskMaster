# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TaskMaster collects and uses personal data when you use the service.

## What we collect

We may collect and store the following data:

- **Account & profile data:** email, nickname, first and last name, pronoun, phone, role/status, and account creation date.
- **Authentication data:** email verification tokens, session tokens, and related session metadata.
- **Membership data:** membership status, expiry date, and subscription/payment references.
- **Event & task data:** event participation, reservations, hosted events, assigned/reviewed tasks, and skill badges.
- **Orders & purchases:** order and order-item details, totals, VAT amounts, payment request IDs, and payee references.
- **Communications:** newsletter consent, newsletter send-out data, and transactional emails (e.g., sign-in links and order updates).
- **Content & uploads:** info pages, text content/translations, and uploaded images/files (stored as URLs).

## How we use your data

We use your data to:

- Provide and operate the service (accounts, memberships, events, tasks, and orders).
- Authenticate users and secure access.
- Process payments and manage subscriptions.
- Send transactional emails and newsletters (when you’ve opted in).
- Improve service reliability and user experience.

## Sharing and processors

We share data only with service providers necessary to run TaskMaster, such as:

- **Payment processing:** Swedbank Pay (payment execution; we do not store card details).
- **Email delivery:** SMTP/Nodemailer-compatible email provider.
- **File storage:** Vercel Blob for uploaded files.

These providers process data on our behalf and are required to protect it.

## Cookies

We use cookies required for authentication and session management. These are essential to sign in and keep you logged in.

## Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized where possible.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. Contact us through the site’s contact channels to make a request.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
